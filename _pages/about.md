A optimization and control background-turned AI person. I am currently working at Xsense.ai on deep learning based planning in mapless environment. I obtained my Ph.D. from the University of Michigan in control and optimization. I have worked s a research intern at [Mitsubishi Electric Research Laboratories](https://www.merl.com/). 

Currently, My research focuses on developing generalizable machine learning methods for decision making with an application to autonomous driving planning in mapless environment. During my Ph.D. study, I was working on electrified vehicle control and optimization.
